# Canonical Product Vision

## Working name

# Life Atlas

Internal engine name: **Living Capability Engine**

## Core promise

Life Atlas helps a person understand:

> How am I developing over time, what patterns are shaping that development, and what should I do next?

## What makes it different

Most products measure isolated activities. Life Atlas models behavioural order, recurrence, timing, context, short-term state, long-term conditioning, cross-domain interactions, progress toward aspirations, evidence quality, and confidence.

## Core loop

```text
Behaviour
→ Sequence
→ Pattern
→ Temporary State
→ Capability or Risk
→ Atlas Change
→ Recommendation
→ New Behaviour
```

## Positioning

> A longitudinal human-development platform.

## Safety boundary

Life Atlas must not present inferred behavioural states as medical or psychological diagnoses. It may identify patterns and recommend professional support where appropriate, while making limitations clear.
