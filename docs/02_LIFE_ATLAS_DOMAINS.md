# Life Atlas Domains

## 1. Health
Sleep stability, recovery, activity, nutrition consistency, energy regulation, pain-aware behaviour, adherence support, physical resilience.

## 2. Psychological Functioning
Emotional regulation, stress resilience, self-awareness, decision fatigue, focus, motivation stability, adaptive thinking, confidence, cognitive recovery, self-efficacy.

## 3. Social Capability
Communication, empathy, conflict management, relationship maintenance, collaboration, social confidence, boundaries, community participation, support seeking, reciprocity.

## 4. Happiness and Flourishing
Positive affect, satisfaction, restorative experiences, enjoyment, connection, autonomy, balance, gratitude, fulfilment, sustainable pleasure.

## 5. Risk Mitigation
Overload, burnout-pattern risk, injury risk, financial vulnerability, social isolation, safety compliance, poor recovery, cognitive overload, recurring self-defeating loops.

## 6. Professional Development
Reliability, planning, initiative, communication, collaboration, leadership, learning, judgement, strategic thinking, organisation, adaptability, domain mastery, career readiness.

## 7. Purpose and Growth
Values alignment, meaning, curiosity, creativity, contribution, identity development, achievement, learning orientation, mentoring, aspiration development.

## Cross-domain ecology

```text
Poor sleep
→ lower focus
→ poorer planning
→ higher stress
→ more conflict
→ weaker recovery
→ poorer sleep
```
