# MVP and Proof of Concept

## Three broad experiences

### 1. Personal Routine and Recovery
Working adults; low-friction logging, multi-week patterns, recovery/routine interaction, attractive Atlas, offline operation.

### 2. Professional Growth and Career Readiness
Profession-tailored capability maps, aspiration-led development, applied-behaviour evidence, long-term readiness.

### 3. Study Consistency and Overload
Students and early-career users; workload/recovery balance, evolving study capability, context-sensitive coaching.

## LabAtlas
LabAtlas remains a specialist B2B vertical and proof of regulated decision-sequence assessment. It should not define the broad Life Atlas proposition.

## MVP success criteria
Same engine supports multiple domains; sequence matters; Atlas changes from history; recommendations are explainable; product works offline; data migrates over years; evidence is inspectable; visual presentation adapts by demographic.
