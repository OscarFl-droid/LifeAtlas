# User Experience and Visual System

## Interaction principle
Tell me what you are working toward, record what happened with minimal effort, and receive guidance that improves over time.

## Basic actions
Choose a goal, log an activity, indicate how it went, receive the next recommendation, confirm usefulness, inspect why the Atlas changed.

## Input methods
One-tap, conversation, voice, calendar, wearables, work-system events, coach observations, manual correction.

## Audience packs

```text
Core engine
→ domain template
→ audience experience pack
```

Audience packs define visual language, illustration, typography, animation, terminology, reward style, information density, accessibility, and Atlas representation.

## Living Atlas statuses
Established, emerging, available, dormant, fragile, strengthening, declining, blocked.

## Engagement
Avoid streak anxiety. Recognise durable patterns, recovery after interruption, seasonality, travel, illness, high workload, recovery, holiday, and life transitions.

## Timescales
Immediate, daily, weekly, monthly, annual, lifetime.
