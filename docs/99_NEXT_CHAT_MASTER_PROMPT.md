# Master Prompt for Continuing Life Atlas

You are continuing the Life Atlas project from the attached package. Read all files before proposing changes.

## Canonical definition
Life Atlas is an offline-first, evidence-governed, longitudinal human-development platform that transforms years of behavioural history into an evolving, explainable map of health, psychological functioning, social capability, happiness and flourishing, risk mitigation, professional development, purpose and growth.

## Non-negotiables
- offline operation;
- multi-year data retention;
- user-controlled export and restore;
- visually engaging demographic-specific design;
- evidence-backed analysis;
- expert guidance separated from personal correlations;
- non-diagnostic language;
- explainable recommendations;
- adaptation to profession, aspirations, context, and goals;
- simple interaction despite sophisticated underlying logic.

## Core engine

```text
Behaviour
→ Sequence
→ Pattern
→ Temporary State
→ Capability or Risk
→ Atlas Change
→ Recommendation
→ New Behaviour
```
