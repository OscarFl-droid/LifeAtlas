# Evidence Governance and Recommendation Safety

## Recommendation classes
A. Safety and professional-standard rules
B. Evidence-based population guidance
C. Individual pattern findings
D. Experimental suggestions

## Evidence hierarchy
1. Authoritative guidelines
2. Systematic reviews and meta-analyses
3. High-quality controlled studies
4. Strong prospective studies
5. Formal expert consensus
6. Individual peer-reviewed studies used cautiously
7. Personal correlations clearly labelled

## Governance roles
Domain lead, evidence reviewer, safety reviewer, behavioural-design reviewer, product editor, review owner.

## AI role
AI may structure input, summarise patterns, adapt explanations, help experts author templates, retrieve approved evidence, and draft coaching language. It must not invent unrestricted clinical, safety, or professional advice.
