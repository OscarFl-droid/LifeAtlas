# Life Atlas – Continuation Package

This package contains the working concept, architecture, product logic, design principles, commercial framing, and a cleaned project transcript sufficient to continue the Life Atlas project in a new chat.

## Canonical definition

**Life Atlas is an offline-first, evidence-governed, longitudinal human-development platform that transforms years of behavioural history into an evolving, explainable map of health, psychological functioning, social capability, happiness, risk mitigation, professional development, and personally chosen growth.**

It is not merely a habit tracker, wellness dashboard, competency framework, journal, or diagnostic tool.

Its differentiator is that behavioural sequence, repetition, timing, context, and accumulated history alter future capability and recommendation relevance.
